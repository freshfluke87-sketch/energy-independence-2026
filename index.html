How to Choose a Solar Generator for Ontario

When the grid goes down in Ontario, you need more than just a battery; you need a system that handles temperature fluctuations. We recommend LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries because they last 10+ years and handle cold-weather storage significantly better than older lithium-ion models.

Key Specs to Watch: Always look for a "Pure Sine Wave" inverter. This ensures that sensitive electronics like your laptop or medical devices aren't damaged by "dirty" power during a blackout.
